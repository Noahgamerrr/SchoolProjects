# TaTü Management
SYP 2023/24 - Team A

## Use-case
This project serves the purpose of simplifying management of the open days at HTL Villach.  
Its primary purpose is the organization of guides, stations and station workers in a simple and intuitive manner.
For further reference, please refer to [the requirements document](./TaTü%20planning/requirements.md).

## Main goal
Deployment of the application at the HTL Villach open day 2025
