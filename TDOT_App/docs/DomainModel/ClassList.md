# Classes

> **EDITORS NOTE** > _Guides and station worker classes need information about the time they are working as that job. For example a guide from 9 am to 10 am and a station worker from 10 to 11:30 am_

### People

---

-   **Student**
    _Describes an imported student without a task_
-   **Guide**
    _A student assigned with guiding visitors_
-   **Team Leader**
    _A student in charge of a guide team._
-   **Station Worker**
    _A student working in a station_
-   **Station Leader**
    _A student in charge of leading a station_
-   **Teacher**
-   **Organiser**
    _Teacher in charge of organizing the TDOT, capable of creating and removing TDOTs_
-   **Visitor**
    _Someone lead by a guide visiting the various stations_

### Objects

---

-   **Guide Team**
    _A team of several guides and one team leader._
-   **Station**
    _A single TDOT station where a subject is presented_
-   **TDOT**
    _Describes a single TDOT. On a specific date._
-   **Station Review**
    _Describes a review of a station made by a visitor_
-   **Tour**
    _Describes a tour led by a guide_
-   **Timetable**
    _A representation of past breaks and work done by a student_
