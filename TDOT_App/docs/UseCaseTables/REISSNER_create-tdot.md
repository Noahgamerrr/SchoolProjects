|---|---|
| Use Case Package     ||
| Use Case Name        | create TDOT|
| Use Case Description | The creation of the TDOT describes the initialization of the planning phase, which could also be described as the declarition of a TDOT being held within due time|
| Primary Actor        | responsible teacher|
| Actors               | Participating teachers|
| Precondition         | TDOT has yet to be heldin respective year and is planned to be held in said year;\
No TDOT is being held at the time of creation of a new one|
| Postcondition        | TDOT is created and participants may proceed to additional planning|
| Trigger              | The responsible administrator decides for the TDOT to be held within the foreseeable future|
| Basic, normal Flow   | 1. the administrator, with all the conditions upheld, starts the creation of a new TDOT;\
2. All necessary information (year, etc) is double checked\
3. a new TDOT is created after validation of data \
4. administrators eventually proceed to the assignment of stations and data to be kept from the last TDOT|
| alternative Flows    | 1. during the normal flow, the TDOT is, due to unforeseeable circumstances, cancelled\
2. the administrator cancels the creation of the TDOT|
