| Requirement | Description |
|--|--|
| Name | Report abandoned station | 
| ID | 01 |
| Description | It might happen that all station workers went to take a break, so that there is nobody who could present their station to the visitors. In this case, the station needs to be reported to the supervising teacher in order to show every station of the exhibition to the visitors. |
| Primary Actor | Guide |
| Actors | Guide, Station teacher, Station student |
| Preconditions | An active station is left without station worker |
| Postconditions | At least one station worker should end their break and go back to their station |
| Trigger | A guide reports the abandoned station to its respective supervisor. |
| Happy Flow | 1. A guide wants to show a station to the visitor\
2. The guide notices that there is no station worker who could present the station\
3. The guide looks up the station teacher\
4. The guide tells the station teacher about the abandoned station\
5. The station teacher notifies their students and asks who could be free to take on the presentation\
6. A station worker tells the teacher that they're free\
7. The station worker ends their break and goes back to the station\
8. The station worker presents its station to the visitor. |
| Alternative Flow | 1. A guide wants to show a station to the visitor\
2. The guide notices that there is no station worker who could present the station\
3. The guide looks up the station teacher\
4. The guide tells the station teacher about the abandoned station\
5. The station teacher notifies their students and asks who could be free to take on the presentation\
6. No station worker is ready to end their break\
7. The station teacher deliberately demands the station worker with the longest break to end their break\
8. The station worker ends their break and goes back to the station\
9. The station worker presents its station to the visitor.|
